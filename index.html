changing nature of accounting

Accounting is changing rapidly with the new developments in technology, automation, data analytics, machine learning, natural language processing, even OCR/optical character recognition - all of it under one very powerful umbrella of digital innovation. A lot of the changes are still widely misunderstood and cautiously pursued. This blog is a means of exploring a small glimpse of what's possible, the challenges behind it and how programming can influence accounting.
